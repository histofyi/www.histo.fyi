Changelog

v 0.2

Known issues

  • Single chain complexes are currently not categorized - single chain complexes being a combination of peptide, Class I alpha chain and beta 2 microglobulin joined by linkers. e.g. 2QRI
  • Class I alpha chains with a full length sequence in their structure files currently fail on categorization and matching to allele sequences.
  • Early structures, either without discernable peptide or with fragments of modelled peptides are not categorized. e.g. 2HLA
  • Structures with unusual chain lettering, e.g. D-2 will not have those chains coloured correctly in molecular visualisations. e.g. 7RRG
  • 52 structures are currently uncategorised, representing just under 4% of the Class I structures analyzed.
  • Some gamma/delta T cell receptor containing complexes are not-currently characterised, leading to an under representation in the sets.
  • Truncated Class I alpha chains (expressed as antigen binding domains only) are not currently present in the PDBe search query. Future releases will include these by combining a set of different queries. e.g. 6L9K
