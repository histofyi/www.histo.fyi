Welcome to histo.fyi

histo.fyi is a database of 3D structures of MHC molecules and their ligands which are available in the Protein Databank. It provides data about these molecules in a standardised interface, using the most up to date nomenclature.
The initial focus is on MHC Class I molecules. Over time it will be extended to cover MHC Class II molecules and their ligands and non-classical MHC molecules and homologues.
