Dr Chris Thorpe

During his PhD, Chris worked with Dr Paul Travers, one of his PhD supervisors, to build an early website about MHC molecules, histo.cryst.bbk.ac.uk (now sadly defunct).

After a 20 year career break working in the technology sector, Chris has returned to his research in Structural/Computational Immunology and Bioinformatics.

You can follow Chris on Twitter, see his publications on Google Scholar, or see his resume on LinkedIn. His ORCID is 0000-0002-4341-0320.
