Welcome to histo.fyi

histo.fyi is a database of 3D structures of Major Histocompatibility Complex (MHC molecules and their ligands which are available in the Protein Databank. It provides data about these molecules in a standardised interface, using the most up to date nomenclature.
The complex of an antigenic peptide and the MHC molecule (pMHC) is the ligand recognised by T-cell receptors (TCR) as part of the adaptive immune response to pathogens and tumors.
The initial focus of this database/website is on MHC Class I molecules. Over time it will be extended to cover MHC Class II molecules and their ligands and non-classical MHC molecules and homologues.
